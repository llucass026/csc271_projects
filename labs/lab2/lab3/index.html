CACHUPA:TRADITIONAL

DISH OF CAPE VERDE -

FULL RECIPE

Cachupa is Cape Verde's national dish. It's a rich stew of hominy, beans, seasoned meats and vegetables.
Every family has their own version, and even within families, there are different variations.

The Reason why it is my favorite dish is because it's a famous Dish in the country I was born (Cape Verde) and every time I eat this dish it reminds me of home.
