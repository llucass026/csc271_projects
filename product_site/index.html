WHO WE ARE

The story of Walk by Faith began with a vision to inspire believers to live boldly for Christ in every aspect of life, including what they wear.
“For we walk by faith, not by sight,” (2 Corinthians 5:7) Our mission is to create faith-inspired apparel that reminds you to trust God's path daily.
Walk by Faith is more than just clothing it's a movement to declare our identity in Christ and share His love with the world.
Each piece is crafted to reflect the truth and hope we have in Jesus. Together we walk in His light.
